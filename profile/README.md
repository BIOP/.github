# BIOP — BioImaging & Optics Platform

**Open-source tools for biological image analysis and microscopy.**

---

## What We Work On

### QuPath Extensions
We maintain popular extensions for [QuPath](https://qupath.github.io/), including deep-learning segmentation ([Cellpose/Omnipose](https://github.com/BIOP/qupath-extension-cellpose)), image registration ([Warpy](https://github.com/BIOP/qupath-extension-warpy)), spot detection ([Spotiflow](https://github.com/BIOP/qupath-extension-spotiflow)), and brain atlas integration ([ABBA](https://github.com/BIOP/qupath-extension-abba)). Browse them all in our [extension catalog](https://github.com/BIOP/qupath-biop-catalog).

### ImageJ / Fiji Plugins
Wrappers to call [Cellpose, Elastix & Ilastik from Fiji](https://github.com/BIOP/ijl-utilities-wrappers), pyramidal image conversion with [KHEOPS](https://github.com/BIOP/ijp-kheops), [label ↔ ROI conversion](https://github.com/BIOP/ijp-LaRoMe), [Fourier Ring Correlation](https://github.com/BIOP/ijp-frc), an [Imaris–Fiji bridge](https://github.com/BIOP/EasyXT-FIJI), and more.

### Brain Atlas Registration
[ABBA](https://github.com/BIOP/ijp-imagetoatlas) registers 2D brain sections onto 3D reference atlases, with a [Python interface](https://github.com/BIOP/abba_python) and QuPath integration.

### BigDataViewer Ecosystem
Tools for large multi-dimensional datasets: [image loaders](https://github.com/BIOP/bigdataviewer-image-loaders), [visualization tools](https://github.com/BIOP/bigdataviewer-biop-tools), and a [fast CZI reader](https://github.com/BIOP/quick-start-czi-reader).

### Napari Plugins
[Point tracking with CoTracker3](https://github.com/BIOP/napari-cotcotcot).

### Teaching & Outreach
Materials for our [Image Processing & Analysis MOOC](https://github.com/BIOP/IPA4LSx), a [colocalization board game](https://github.com/BIOP/coLoc), and [reusable educational figures](https://github.com/BIOP/BIOP-Educational-Figures).
